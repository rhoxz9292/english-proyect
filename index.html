Is it safe to wear NeuraSound all day?

Absolutely. Our sensors are passive; they only read electrical activity, they do not emit any signals into your brain. It is as safe as wearing a smartwatch.

Does it work with Spotify and Apple Music?

Yes. NeuraSound acts as a control layer over your OS. It works natively with any audio app on iOS and Android.

What happens if I lose focus?

Our AI distinguishes between wandering thoughts and intentional commands. Plus, you can configure sensitivity in the app to prevent accidental changes.
